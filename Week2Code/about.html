About ICT502

You're about to embark on an exciting journey through the world of web design and development. This course is tailored to equip you with the essential skills needed to create impactful, user-friendly websites. From mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to exploring cutting-edge technologies and trends, you'll learn to navigate the digital landscape with confidence.

Remember, this is more than just learning code; it's about unlocking your potential to innovate and connect the world in new ways. Your instructors are here to guide and support you, and we can't wait to see the incredible projects you'll bring to life.

To get started, please download the course materials and follow the instructions provided in the course outline. We're here to help, and we'll be available during your first class session to answer any questions you may have.
